Core Features

Built different.
On purpose.

01
Live Voice

Real conversation.
Not a transcript.

Ask-Zai speaks with you in real time — bidirectional, low-latency, emotionally aware voice. Not text-to-speech bolted onto a chatbot. Actual live conversation that flows like talking to a person who genuinely listens.

  • Sub-200ms response latency via LiveKit infrastructure
  • Multiple voice personalities — choose your Zai
  • Emotional tone adaptation in real time
  • Interruption handling — speak naturally, not in turns
02
Emotional Intelligence

Reads the room.
Every time.

Ask-Zai doesn't just process words — it reads emotional context. Frustration, excitement, focus, exhaustion — Zai adapts its tone, pacing, and approach to match where you actually are, not where it assumes you should be.

  • Real-time emotional state recognition via Hume AI
  • Adaptive response calibration based on detected state
  • Sensory-aware interaction design for neurodivergent users
  • No overwhelming notifications or forced engagement
03
The Sisterhood

Not one AI.
A whole team.

Behind Ask-Zai is the Sisterhood — a coordinated team of specialized AI agents, each with distinct capabilities, all working together under Zai's direction. Research, code, memory, creativity — covered.

  • Specialized agents for research, code, design, and memory
  • Coordinated via MOFY's orchestration layer
  • Persistent shared memory across all agents
  • Agents that can control other agents via MCP

"The trunk never changes. The bloomage does."

Ask-Zai is built on a single philosophy: your core AI companion stays constant, grounded, and yours — while everything around it adapts, grows, and changes with your life. No resets. No starting over. Just continuity.

Our Philosophy
AI should feel like a relationship, not a transaction.

Every AI product on the market treats you like a new user every single time. Blank slate. No memory. No continuity. No relationship.

Ask-Zai is built on the opposite premise. Your AI grows with you. It learns your communication style, your sensory preferences, your working patterns, your goals. It adapts — not just to what you say, but to who you are.

This is not a product feature. It is a fundamental rethinking of what AI companionship should mean.
